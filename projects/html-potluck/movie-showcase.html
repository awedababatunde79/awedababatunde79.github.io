My Movie Showcase

Here are three movies I Like.

Movie Posters

             

Black Panther

Black Panther tells the story of T'Challa, the new king of Wakanda, who must protect his people and fight to defend his kingdom from powerful enemies. The movie is full of action, strong characters, and an important message about leadership and responsibility.

Terminator 3 Rise of the Machines

In "Terminator 3: Rise of the Machines," John Connor faces a new threat from Skynet as a more advanced Terminator, the T-X, is sent back in time to eliminate him and his future allies, while a reprogrammed T-850 is sent to protect them.

The Lion King

The Lion King is about Simba, a young lion who must grow up and learn to take his place as king. The story includes friendship, loss, courage, and family, making it one of the most memorable animated films.
